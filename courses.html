Get Back in the GameÂ®

Introducing the Back in the Game Virtual Course! We have spent the past few years developing an affordable sports performance program for athletes of all ages. Our goal is to teach athletes the necessary mental skills to take their game to the next level! We focus on teaching six core mental skills (goal setting, preparation, confidence building, self-talk/visualization, focus/motivation, working through adversity) that research has shown to lead to game-changing success. In addition, we provide education and prevention strategies to athletes on the topics of mental health and addiction.

Individual Online Course
$399

This option is an 8 module course with personalized assignment feedback, mental skills development and mental health/at-risk behavior education, virtual group monthly, and 3 face to face virtual meetings. At this time, this option is only available for athletes.

Six Individual Sessions
$599

This course option includes six individual sessions, a mental performance assessment, and an individualized mental workout. At this time, this option is available for athletes and performers, and can be tailored to an individual's specific needs.

Personalized Team Coaching
$2,000

This option is a personalized team online course, and includes 3 coaching sessions for coaches and or players (with a minimum of 20 players). This is available for both athletes and performers, with additional team and individual sessions upon request.

Advanced Team Coaching
$4,500

This is an 8-month program which includes a personalized team online course, and monthly team meetings(with a minimum of 20 players). This is available for both athletes and performers, with additional team and individual sessions upon request.
