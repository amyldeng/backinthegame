What is Back in the Game®?

Back in the Game® is a program designed to help performers and athletes develop the mental skills necessary to take their game to the next level. We’ve identified six core mental skills that are critical to developing a mental edge and amplifying talent.

We provide coaching sessions to teams and private lessons to individuals based on these core skills. Back in the Game harnesses game-changing expertise to help athletes out-perform the competition and build on success. Our programs build confidence, increase motivation, and focus and curb anxiety in athletes and performers as they navigate a winning season.
